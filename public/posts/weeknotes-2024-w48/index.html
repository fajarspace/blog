Weeknotes 2024 W48: Not Great, Not Terrible

December 1, 2024
  • Been feeling the classic not-really-ill-not-really-well funk this week, so this is a shorter update.
  • @dpom@fosstodon.org wrote a post on how to get color and undercurl support in Neovim when running in tmux and Alacritty. This is something I have written about before , however, this solution is a bit cleaner. I’ve created a note for easier copy/pasting and future reference.
  • I’ve been watching a few Let’s Play videos on the recently released (sidenote: First-person shooter survival horror video game developed and published by GSC Game World.) game. I’m a big fan of, and used to play, the previous STALKER games, and this one inspired me to pull the trigger on a beefy gaming computer so I can play it myself. I can’t wait to explore The Zone!
